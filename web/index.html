About ORE

ORE/Open Rating Environment is a project aiming to create a platform for enterprise revenue management.

Currently (as of 2004/12/03) the project is in the planning phase. Although we have written a little document explaining formally the motivation and goals of ORE, these are the highlights:

  • Open platform for development and integration based on Free/Libre and Open Source Software and open technologies like Java.
  • Customer/user, services and subscriptions database storage and access API's.
  • Mediation and rating of usage events to charge (rating engine-like functionality).
  • Aggregation, post processing and submission of costs to charging platforms on billing cycles (billing engine-like functionality).
  • Provisioning of customer and subscription data to external systems providing the actual service to the customers.
  • Customizable functionality for rating, billing and other business rules via plug-ins and implementor-editable flow-graphs.
